Role Summary

The Data Quality Assurance Specialist is responsible for ensuring that data is accurate, consistent, and reliable across all of the company’s core business systems. As the company scales, having timely, accurate data is critical to understanding performance and making informed decisions. The Data Quality Assurance Specialist ensures that data across the company’s systems is consistent, reliable, and available for real-time operational and financial analysis.

This role focuses on hands-on data validation, reconciliation, and issue resolution across platforms such as QuickBooks, Salesforce, JIRA, the company’s internally developed operations and inventory systems, and SAP as it is implemented and becomes a core system of record.

This is an individual contributor role that works closely with Sales, Operations, Logistics, Service, Accounting/Finance, and IT to support accurate reporting, effective processes, and scalable system usage.

Key Responsibilities

Data Accuracy & Reconciliation

Review and validate data input by other business partners across core business systems, including QuickBooks, Salesforce, JIRA, and internal operations systems to ensure accuracy and compliance
Investigate discrepancies in core business systems when discovered
Perform routine audits to ensure cleanliness and accuracy of data
Identify patterns and root causes of recurring issues
Correct issues directly or coordinate with system owners to resolve them
Support SAP implementation
Verify that medical aesthetic devices purchased and sold are accurately recorded by make, model, year, and serial number across sales, inventory, and accounting records
Support device traceability by ensuring serial number accuracy for purchased, sold, serviced, and returned medical aesthetic devices

Systems & Workflow Support

Partner with Sales, Operations, Logistics, Service, Accounting/Finance, and IT to resolve system-related data issues
Assist with data cleanup during process changes, system updates, or integrations
Assist with testing and validation during the SAP implementation and post-go-live period

Training & Process Enablement

Help train team members on correct system usage and data entry standards
Reinforce new and existing processes as the business grows and evolves
Create and maintain clear documentation, guides, and reference materials
Act as a go-to resource for questions related to system data and workflows

Reporting & Monitoring

Generate reports and checks to identify data inconsistencies or gaps
Monitor for anomalies that could impact financials, operations, logistics, sales reporting, or service delivery
Proactively flag and escalate data issues that could affect decision-making

Qualifications

1–4 years of experience working with business systems or operational data
Strong attention to detail and comfort working with structured data
Experience with accounting systems, CRM tools, or operational software
Ability to follow data across multiple systems and understand end-to-end workflows
Strong communication skills and comfort working cross-functionally

Preferred Skills

Experience with QuickBooks, Salesforce, JIRA, or similar platforms
Strong Excel skills to evaluate data and detect issues
Exposure to ERP systems (SAP, NetSuite, Oracle, etc.)
Experience supporting growing teams or scaling processes

What Success Looks Like

Leadership and teams trust real-time operational and financial data
Data issues are identified early and resolved at the root cause
New hires are trained on correct system usage quickly and consistently
Business decisions are supported by accurate, reliable data as the company scales
